What is equine assisted counselling?

You don't need to know anything about horses

Equine assisted counselling combines the skill of a trained therapist with the natural sensitivity of horses. Sessions take place outdoors, working alongside the horses — not riding them. There is no experience required and nothing to prepare.

Horses are honest, present and non-judgmental. They respond to how you are feeling in the moment, not how you think you should be feeling. For many people, this creates a kind of connection that is difficult to find in a traditional therapy room — and makes it easier to open up.

Sessions are calm, unhurried and led entirely by you, with me guiding and supporting at every step.
