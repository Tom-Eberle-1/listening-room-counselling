About the practice

The team

At The Listening Room, you are never alone in a room. You are met by people and animals who create a space of calm, honesty and connection.

Founder & Lead Counsellor

Michelle Welch

Michelle founded The Listening Room Counselling Service with a belief that meaningful therapeutic work can happen anywhere — and that some of the most profound conversations happen outdoors, in the company of animals.

She is a fully accredited counsellor with many years of experience working with adults and young people in private practice and school environments. She offers individual counselling and equine assisted therapy from the practice near Hawkstone Park Hotel and Golf Club.

Michelle works with people navigating anxiety, depression, grief, relationship difficulties, life transitions, and more — always at your pace, always without judgment.

How we work

Our approach

Person-centred

We work at your pace, in a way that feels right for you. There is no fixed script — every session is shaped around your needs.

Nature-based

We believe the outdoors has a unique power to support emotional wellbeing. Our equine sessions take full advantage of working in a natural environment.

Non-judgmental

Whatever you bring to a session, you will be met with honesty, warmth and complete confidentiality.

Experienced

Michelle has worked extensively with adults and young people across private practice and school settings throughout Shropshire.
